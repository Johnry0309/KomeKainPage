Korean × Filipino

At KomeKain, every bite is a meeting of cultures. From our Spam Musubi layered with golden omelette, creamy Kewpie mayo, and a sweet-savory teriyaki glaze,

to our one-of-a-kind Pastil Onigiri—fusing Filipino comfort with Japanese tradition—we craft flavors that feel both familiar and new.

Made fresh daily with the warmth of home cooking, our food is more than just a meal—it’s a story of family, heritage, and bold creativity. Whether you’re here for a quick bite or a taste of something unforgettable, we wrap happiness into every musubi and onigiri.
